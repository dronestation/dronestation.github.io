A solution for charging drones wirelessly

Battery charging technologies have remained unchanged for the last 20 years and are not ready for charging efficiently flying robots (drones). Current technologies rely on correct user handling, are static, not interoperable and have high environmental impact.

The dronestation is the next generation technology for charging drones wirelessly using green energy and sustainable technologies. We’ve created a product that would allow users to charge their flying devices in a secure and seamless way anywhere, everywhere and anytime.
